Key Findings
Finding 1 — H1

Pain Signals Are Universal Across Clarivate Products

11 of 14 emotional pain clusters span 5+ of 9 product domains. Users across DI&A, CPI, MT360, KAI, MASS, PT, CDDI, OFFX, Fusion feel more alike than different. This was invisible at per-project scope.

Finding 2 — H4

Pain Intensity Must Gate Confidence Weighting

Blind frequency-based confidence weighting incorrectly demotes high-stakes, rare signals. MENA/Brazil regional coverage (pain 9.0/10, only 3 users) is a P0 churn risk — blind weighting makes it P4. Rule: pain ≥ 8.0 → confidence floor = 0.9, regardless of frequency.

Finding 3 — H8

Per-Product AI Roadmaps Undervalue Platform Investments

When H1 cluster universality weights are applied to 24 AI opportunities: 22 re-rank, 4 product-specific items demote. The result reveals 3 platform capabilities — Data Synthesis, Trust/RAG Infrastructure, NL Workflow Automation — each addressable with shared platform code rather than per-product builds.

Finding 4 — H1 + H9

UMAP Dramatically Outperforms PCA for Empathy Clustering

Switching from PCA to UMAP reduces clustering noise from 49% → 12% on the same dataset. The empathy-clustering skill (H9) makes this accessible with a single command: /empathy-clustering [project]. Runtime: <10 seconds per project.
